Yoga & Meditation Hall

A serene, open-air space dedicated to the practice of Yoga and Meditation under the guidance of expert instructors. Daily sessions are conducted to harmonize the mind and body.

In-house Pharmacy and Production

We prepare our own authentic medicines using fresh herbs from our garden. Our pharmacy is stocked with a wide range of Kashayams, Arishtams, and Lehyams ensuring quality and purity.

Hygienic Treatment Rooms

Our therapy rooms are designed for comfort and hygiene, featuring traditional Dhroni tables and attached steam bath facilities. Each room provides a private and calming environment.
